TheLocalCart brings you a refined hyperlocal shopping experience. We ensure excellent quality, fast delivery and a platform for hyperlocal business development.

What we do

We provide a smooth and refined connection between customers and local sellers without much involvement from a third party. We provide cheap delivery options and give the kind of flexibility offered by none other. We also provide a platform for small businesses to grow their customer base.

We were inspired by the Vocal for Local campaign started by our honorable Prime Minister. This project is a small effort to boost India's economy by encouraging local sellers and helping home grown businesses to get of their feet. Such encouragement will give rise to a new generation of businesses and move India closet to a self reliant future.
